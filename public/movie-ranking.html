My Favourite Feel-Good Movies!

What I often watch

Dr. Dolittle

A movie about a doctor who aquires the ability to talk to animals, this movie is perfect for nights you don't want to go to bed, but do not want to exhaust your mind.

Spirited Away

A movie about the adventures of a little girl who gets trapped in a mysterious world with her parents, the movie will sure get a chuckle out of you.

Legally Blonde

A perfect movie. Shows you that you can reinvent yourself at any time, without having to give up what you like.
